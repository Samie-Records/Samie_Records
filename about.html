About

Founded by Kolawole Samuel Adewalure, professionally known as Samie, this platform was created to fill a noticeable gap in the music industry — the lack of high-quality, authentic Afrobeat samples. As a seasoned music producer and sound engineer with years of hands-on experience, I’ve made it my mission to craft premium sounds that reflect the true essence of Afrobeat and empower other creators to bring their musical visions to life.
